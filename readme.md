# Ruqayya UGC Beauty Creator Portfolio

A premium portfolio website for Ruqayya, a UGC Beauty Creator specializing in cinematic aesthetic content for skincare, beauty, and jewelry brands.

## About

Ruqayya creates scroll-stopping UGC that feels like luxury editorial content. With soft natural lighting, clean compositions, and genuine on-camera energy, she transforms products into irresistible visual stories. Based in Lagos with global reach, her minimal yet cinematic aesthetic is perfect for brands seeking authentic content that converts.

## Services

- **Product Videos**: Vertical cinematic videos with perfect lighting and natural voiceovers
- **Product Photography**: Studio-grade flat lays, hero shots, and macro details
- **Unboxing Videos**: Premium ASMR-style unboxings with elegant reveals
- **Aesthetic Content**: Minimal editorial-style videos and photos
- **Lifestyle Content**: Relatable on-camera lifestyle shots

## Installation & Usage

1. Clone or download the repository
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. The site is fully static and requires no build process

## Customization

To customize this portfolio:

1. Update the hero stats and content in `index.html`
2. Replace placeholder images in the HTML with your actual photos/videos
3. Update social media links and contact information
4. Modify colors and styling in `styles/style.css`
5. Replace the Formspree form ID with your own
